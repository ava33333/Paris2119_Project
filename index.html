Paris 2119 Project

This website is based on the bande desinée (BD) "Paris 2119" by Zep. The book, which was written in 2019, explores the topics of technology and authoritarianism. My website demonstrates how important it is to properly implement technology, especially when it will be heavily integrated into the infrastructure of society.

In "Paris 2119", there is a teleportation device called the "Transcore", which most individuals use as their main form of transportation. Through each demonstration, I show how technology, like the Transcore, is a tool. As a society, it is essential to ensure the most ethical use of powerful technology, along with full transparency of its limitations. Though this was written before artificial intelligence became widely accessible and popular, Zep presents an important idea that is relevant considering the current exponential growth of AI: technology is neutral, but it is necessary to ensure it is used ethically.

This website is based on an open-ended final project for my Francophone Cultures course on bande desinées at College of Charleston. It is set up as an interactive demonstration, which slowly develops my thesis, similar to how a French paper would be laid out.

The best way to experience this website is by clicking through each page in the order I provide below. You can also check out each page individually by navigating to the menu on the top left of each page.

Page order:

  • Ship of Theseus demo: a demonstration on the philosophy behind the Ship of Theseus & the connections it has to the BD
  • Transcore Simulation: a demonstration on how the Transcore works and its limitations
  • MIF Archive: a page that looks like a futuristic French government page, which displays the overly authoritarian Paris 2119 government in action. Note: There is no overt button to go to the next page. You must find it.
  • Historical Connections: a page on the connections between real French/global history and the BD, as well as how the perspective this BD gives is important to discuss with the rise of powerful technology like AI
