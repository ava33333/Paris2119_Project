# Paris 2119 — Interactive Digital Essay

**FRCS 102 Final Project** | College of Charleston

A GitHub Pages site built as an interactive digital essay analyzing *Paris 2119* by Zep. The project explores how the bande dessinée frames technology not as inherently dangerous, but as a surface onto which power concentration, institutional secrecy, and blind trust become visible — anxieties with deep roots in French cultural history.

---

## Thesis

Technology is neutral. The danger in *Paris 2119* lies in who controls it, what they conceal, and who chooses not to question it.
